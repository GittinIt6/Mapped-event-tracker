What is the Mapped Event Tracker?

Thanks for checking our app out! This app is designed to show what natural events are currently happening around the world with only a couple of clicks required.

How to find a specific location?

In the top left corner, you'll find a search bar that can be used to search for any city in the world.

How to look for specific eventsin a timeframe?

In the upper right hand corner, you'll find a menu button that will allow you to adjust the events you would like to see appear on the map, as well as the timeframe that those events occured in.
